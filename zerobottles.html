What Is Your Products Sustainable Story?

With ZeroBottles, its a story of responsibility. As the first certified carbon-offset packaging solution for the nutraceutical and pharmaceutical industries, each bottle and cap is paired with climate action that offsets its full carbon footprintfrom creation to deliverythrough verified projects that deliver measurable results.

Manufactured in HDPE, PET, PP, or PE plastics, ZeroBottles match your products needs while maintaining full compliance, performance, and cost efficiency, and can even be crafted with up to 30% post-consumer recycled content to meet upcoming regulations and retailer requirements. Backed by ClimatePartner, every bottle carries a traceable impactreal projects, real results. Its not just sustainableits accountable.

3 Things You Need to Know about ZeroBottles

1.

Your Customers Are Paying Attention

Todays consumers are prioritizing sustainabilityover 70% say they prefer products with eco-friendly packaging. Retailers are following suit, requiring more sustainable options on their shelves. ZeroBottles makes it easy to meet these expectations without compromising performance or profit.

2.

The First of Its Kind

ZeroBottles are the first carbon-neutral plastic bottle engineered for the nutraceutical and pharmaceutical industries. Its fully recyclable, retail-ready, and performs flawlessly with your current production equipment.

3.

Brands are Switching

Customers reward sustainable choices with loyalty, retailers favor eco-forward packaging, margins stay protected with no green tax, every bottle is certified carbon neutral, and integration is seamless with your existing lines.

More Things to Know about ZeroBottles

Does ZeroBottles maintain performance?

Yes. The bottle delivers the same safety, functionality, and cost as standard plastic.

Does it require operational changes?

No. ZeroBottles works on existing equipment and supply chains without reformulation or delays.

Are ZeroBottles more expensive than standard bottles?

Bottles can be produced with 30% PCR content, aligning with emerging recycled content regulations.

Does ZeroBottles meet regulatory standards?

Yes. The 30% PCR option supports compliance with new and upcoming government mandates.

Is ZeroBottles certified carbon neutral?

Yes. Each bottle is ClimatePartner certified and offset through wind and solar projects.

Why is certification important?

60% of consumers only trust sustainability claims with third-party verification, making certification essential for credibility and protection against greenwashing.

Will customers pay more for sustainable packaging?

Yes. 64% of global consumers say theyre willing to pay more, especially when the price difference is small. On average, shoppers accept a 9.7% premium for sustainable goods.

How do eco-labels impact sales?

Products with verified sustainability badges see higher sales, stronger pricing, improved visibility on platforms like Amazon, and easier acceptance at major retailers like Whole Foods.

Why do leading brands make the switch?

To stay ahead of regulations, build customer loyalty, win shelf space, and meet retailer sustainability criteria.

Is ZeroBottles more expensive than standard bottles?

No. ZeroBottles is cost-neutral compared to traditional plastic.

Can it integrate with my supply chain?

Yes. It is designed for seamless compatibility with existing processes and equipment.

What do consumer insights show about climate-friendly products?

45% of shoppers prioritize products with strong climate initiatives, 38% actively search for them, 60% see transparency in climate action as important when choosing products, and 48% believe climate labeling shows a company is taking responsibility.

What is ZeroBottles
PCR+?

A next-generation option that combines 30% PCR content, carbon neutrality, and verified eco-labels retail-ready with no operational changes.
