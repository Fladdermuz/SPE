Join the War on Microplastics

Every year, over 100 billion plastic bottles and caps are produced around the world. Each one can take more than 450 years to break down—and when they do, they leave harmful microplastics behind. These particles are now found in the air we breathe, the water we drink, and the food we eat.

Protect your customers and transition your packaging to solutions that don’t leave microplastics behind.

BioBottles®

BioBottles® are manufactured from traditional resins—HDPE, PE, PP, and PCR—that have been enhanced with our proprietary Plastic IQ® technology. This means they look, feel, and perform just like conventional plastic bottles during manufacturing, storage, and everyday use.

The difference comes at end of life: if BioBottles® enter the environment as pollution, Plastic IQ® activates to accelerate their breakdown in a way that does not leave behind harmful microplastics.

Completely FDA food-grade compliant and shelf-stable for over five years, BioBottles® deliver the durability brands require with the sustainability consumers demand.

ZeroBottles™

ZeroBottles™ are the first certified carbon-offset packaging solution designed for the nutraceutical and pharmaceutical industries. Each bottle and cap is manufactured to neutralize its environmental footprint through verified carbon offset projects—giving your brand a measurable and credible way to reduce impact without changing your supply chain.
